Company Profile

About PACA Energy

PACA Energy Services Limited is a Nigerian company established to provide construction, facilities maintenance, and project management services to the manufacturing and oil and gas industries in Nigeria.

Our Mission

To become the foremost, genuinely indigenous provider of energy, oil, and gas services.

Our Vision

To be a reference company for true local content development in the Nigerian oil and gas industry.

Core Values

Integrity Honesty, diligence, and reliability are the basis of all business. We strive for success by upholding this integrity.
People Our people are our greatest strength. We invest heavily in focused career development and intensive training programs.
QHSE We take QHSE seriously. We ensure total compliance to relevant regulations with a goal of Zero Incidents.
Customer Satisfaction Our customer-focused values drive our determination for integrity and excellence.

Our Management System

Aligned with the ISO 9001:2015 standard.

  • Supports procurement, construction, maintenance, and technical service activities in line with relevant codes and standards.
  • Maintains effective Document Control System in line with ISO 9001:2015.
  • Efficient and proactive HSE Management System designed to prevent injuries with a project goal of Zero Incidents.
  • Implementation of proactive measures including Job Hazard Analysis (JHA), toolbox meetings, and provision of PPE.
